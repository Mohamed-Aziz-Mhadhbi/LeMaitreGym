Weight lifting

Some people may be inclined to some types of sports that are strenuous on the body, such as weightlifting, bodybuilding or gymnastics, without realizing that these sports, if practiced randomly without the supervision of a trainer, or without gradual progress in their exercises, may lead to dire physical results. It is known that the most strenuous and dangerous of these sports among the former is weightlifting; This is because the body needs many exercises that prepare it to accept such a sport, in addition to special care for food and amino acids

Running

Running is a type of physical exercise that moves the muscles and makes them in continuous movement, leading to strengthening them and increasing their mass. It is important in all cases and for all ages, from young to adults and the elderly. Running is also important for stimulating blood circulation, as it is one of the reasons that prevent and alleviate diseases

Among the benefits of running :
. Getting a complete and harmonious body
. Strengthening muscles and increasing muscle mass
